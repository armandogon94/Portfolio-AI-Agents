# Draft — How AI agents are changing customer support in 2026

Tier-1 support has been the punching bag of enterprise CX for a decade.
In 2026, it's finally becoming an agent problem — but only if the
escalation is engineered right.

## Where agents actually win

They deflect the repetitive 60-70%: password resets, invoice lookups,
status checks. They also do something less discussed: they *summarize*
tickets before a human picks them up, which cuts average handle time
by 20% at the teams that measure it.

## Where they still fail

Novelty. Emotion past a threshold. Legal or compliance ambiguity.
Every team I talk to has a cautionary tale about an agent that
cheerfully promised a refund outside policy.

## A 2026 reference architecture

Front tier-1 with an agent; route on a deterministic policy; keep a
human loop for escalations; feed outcomes back to the agent nightly.
That's it. The discipline is what's hard.

## Start here

Audit your top 100 ticket topics against this architecture this quarter.
Most teams find that fewer than 20 drive 80% of volume — a perfect
first target.
